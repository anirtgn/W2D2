Cobb Salad

The Cobb salad is a main-dish American garden salad made from chopped salad greens, tomato, crisp bacon, boiled, grilled or roasted chicken breast, hard-boiled egg, avocado, chives, Roquefort cheese, and red-wine vinaigrette.

One way to remember the components is to use the mnemonic EAT COBB: Egg, Avocado, Tomato, Chicken, Onion, Bacon, Blue cheese - then add lettuce.
